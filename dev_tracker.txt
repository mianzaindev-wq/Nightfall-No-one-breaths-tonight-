## [SESSION 1] Phase 0 - Intro Animation Fixes
**Goal:** Separate the intro animation from the main menu and add cinematic effects.

## [SESSION 2] Phase 1 - The Grand Audit
**Goal:** Deep scan of the codebase to recommend improvements for depth and UI/UX.
- **Action:** Read all core JS and CSS files (`game.js`, `canvas.js`, `network.js`, etc.).
- **Error Found (Pre-existing Bug):** `game.js` calls `sendTo()` inside the kill overlap warning, but `network.js` only exposes `relay()`. 
- **Resolution Planned:** Will replace with `this.net.relay(data, playerId)` in Stage 1 implementation.
- **Error Found (Pre-existing Bug):** Avatar grid rendering twice due to deprecated `arguments.callee`.
- **Resolution Planned:** Will delete the redundant render block in Stage 1.
- **Outcome:** Generated `improvement_audit.md` (33 items).

## [SESSION 3] Phase 2 & 3 - Expansions and Loopholes
**Goal:** Refine the Season 2 mechanics, integrate user ideas, and patch design exploits.
- **Action:** Added dynamic difficulty tiers, Manor Map (26 rooms/3 floors), 12 QTEs, and 8 Role Abilities.
- **Error (Design Flaw):** Originally planned permanent destruction for decaying evidence after 6 rounds. User flagged this as too punishing (old evidence should have *some* value).
- **Resolution:** Reverted permanent destruction. Evidence now decays to a hard floor of 20% accuracy ceiling. Still verifiable, just very weak.
- **Error (Design Exploit):** Killers could potentially abuse Evidence Destroy to guarantee they burn their own forged evidence. 
- **Resolution:** Made Evidence Destroy completely blind (killers don't see which is their own forge) AND made Forging mutually exclusive with Destroying (can't do both in one match).
- **Error (Design Exploit):** Ghost Clue was free-text, allowing dead players to just type the killer's name.
- **Resolution:** Changed Ghost Clue to a drop-down selection of pre-defined vague phrases ("Watch the votes", "Wrong floor", etc.).
