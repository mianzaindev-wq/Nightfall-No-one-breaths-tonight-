# 📖 NIGHTFALL — The Official Gameplay Guide

> _"The clock strikes twelve. The lights go out. The screams begin."_

Welcome to **NightFall**, a real-time, high-stakes multiplayer murder mystery! In this manor, trust is a currency you can't afford, and survival depends entirely on your deduction skills, your mechanical reflexes, and your ability to deceive.

This guide contains everything you need to survive the night—or ensure that no one else does.

---

## 🎭 Roles, Abilities & Limitations

At the start of each match, the manor secretly assigns you a **Role**, a **Persona** (e.g., _The Clockmaker_, _The Serpent_), and a randomly generated **Character Name**. Your character comes with hidden traits—like a specific _Perfume_, _Mark_, _Walk Style_, or _Habit_—that can be used to identify you if you're careless!

Each role comes equipped with **Season 2 Abilities**, which are complete game-changers if used right. **Most abilities can only be used ONCE per match** and require passing a stressful Quick Time Event (QTE).

### 🗡 The Killer

**"Leave no trace. Leave no survivors."**
Your goal is simple: eliminate the innocent until the killers equal or outnumber the living.

- **Action (Night):** Hunt your prey. You must complete a Kill QTE to murder someone. Beware: the QTE gets harder and faster with _every_ successful kill! Fail it, and your victim survives while you drop massive physical evidence linking back to your traits.
- **🔥 Ability 1: Evidence Destroy (Night | Burn QTE | 1 Use):** Blindly select a piece of evidence from the ledger to incinerate. If you fail the QTE, the evidence survives, and you leave scorch marks indicating your exact floor! _Limitation: You cannot use this if you've already forged evidence this match._
- **🎭 Ability 2: Disguise (Night | No QTE | 1 Use):** Activate this to fool the detectives. If you are investigated tonight, their results will falsely clear you as a harmless "Civilian".

### 🔍 The Detective

**"The truth is written in the blood. You just have to read it."**
You are the town's ultimate weapon. Find the killers and lead the execution.

- **Action (Night):** Investigate suspects to uncover their roles or hidden traits. Because of your extensive training, your Investigation QTEs are more forgiving than a Civilian's.
- **📡 Ability 1: Wiretap (Night | No QTE | 1 Use):** Hack into the killers' secure frequency. You will intercept one randomly selected, anonymized message sent in the secret Killer Team Chat!
- **🧩 Ability 2: Cold Case Reopen (Investigate | Jigsaw QTE | 1 Use):** Evidence decays over time, but you never forget. Reconstruct a heavily decayed piece of evidence, restoring its accuracy ceiling back up to 80%. Fail, and the puzzle pieces remain broken.

### 🧑 The Civilian

**"Keep your head down, lock your door, and pray for dawn."**
Standard townsfolk. You have no inherent powers, but your numbers are your strength.

- **Action (Night/Investigate):** You can investigate players, but your QTEs are terrifyingly fast and complex because you are an amateur under extreme stress.
- **🌙 Ability 1: Night Activity (Night | QTE Required | 2 Uses):** You get two charges per match to protect yourself or gather intel:
  - **👂 Listen:** Strain your ears against the wall. You might hear violent struggles in adjacent rooms!
  - **🚪 Barricade:** Lock your door securely, granting up to a 40% chance to block a killer's attack entirely.
  - **🙏 Pray:** Seek divine intervention for a cryptic hint about the killer's traits (but beware, the spirits might sometimes lie).
- **🔭 Ability 2: Town Watch (Investigate | Spotlight QTE | 1 Use):** Survey your surroundings. Count exactly how many night actions (kills, barricades, patrols) occurred on your floor the previous night.

### 🩺 The Doctor (Optional)

**"Do no harm... unless it's an autopsy."**
The selfless healer, tasked with keeping the prime suspects and detectives alive.

- **Action (Night):** Choose a player to protect from being murdered. _Limitation: You cannot self-protect two nights in a row!_
- **🩺 Ability 1: Hallway Patrol (Night | No QTE | Unlimited Uses):** Secure a hallway covering 2-3 adjacent rooms. If anyone inside that hallway gets attacked, you automatically save them! _Limitation: You cannot patrol the exact same hallway twice in a row._
- **🔬 Ability 2: Autopsy (Investigate | Slider QTE | 1 Use):** Examine a fresh corpse. A perfect QTE reveals up to 2 of the victim's hidden traits. A poor QTE leaves you with a vague, inconclusive hunch.

### 🤡 The Jester (Optional)

**"I just want to watch the world burn... from the gallows."**
An agent of pure chaos. You win the game _only_ if the town executes you during the Day Phase!

- **Action:** None natively—your only weapon is your silver tongue.
- **🐊 Ability 1: Crocodile Tears (Night | No QTE | 1 Use):** Fake a global "saved by the doctor" announcement for yourself the next morning, instantly making you the center of attention (and suspicion).

## 🔄 The Continuous Gameplay Loop

NightFall never stops. A single match cycles through these structured, rapid-fire phases until the bitter end.

### 1. 🕰 The Grace Phase (15 Seconds)

_The evening begins._
Players mingle in the **Public Chat**, review the Town Board, and familiarize themselves with their room locations and neighbors. Use this time wisely before the lights go out.

### 2. 🌑 The Night Phase (Lights Out, 30-45 Seconds)

_Darkness swallows the manor._
The primary action phase where words mean nothing and reflexes mean everything.

- **Killers** pick targets to murder, disguise themselves, or burn evidence.
- **Doctors** lock down hallways.
- **Detectives** intercept wiretaps or investigate suspects.
- **Civilians** barricade their doors, pray, or listen in fear.
- **⚡ The QTEs:** Every action you take requires completing a Quick Time Event—hitting a sequence of keys (`W, A, S, D` or arrows) before a timer expires. Fail the QTE, and your action fails!
- **🌪 Night Events:** The weather might turn on you! Events like _Dense Fog_ or a _Thunderstorm_ can dynamically alter QTE difficulty or clue generation in real-time.

### 3. 🔦 The Investigation Phase (Lights On, 40 Seconds)

_Dawn breaks. The horrors are revealed._

- **Chat is Disabled:** It's dead silent. You must piece together your own findings before discussing.
- **The Announcements:** Who died? Who was saved? All blood is laid bare.
- **The Crime Scene:** Any sloppy evidence dropped by Killers is posted to the ledger completely unverified.
- **Investigate Abilities:** This is the phase where Doctors perform **Autopsies**, Detectives **Reopen Cold Cases**, and Civilians use **Town Watch**.

### 4. ☀️ The Dinner / Day Phase (60 Seconds)

_Accusations fly. Trust shatters._

- **Discussion:** All surviving players use the **Public Chat** to debate, share verified evidence, and manipulate the narrative.
- **Voting:** Point the finger! You must vote on a suspect to place on trial. Use the **Suspicion Heatmap** to track voting patterns and hidden alliances.
- **⏩ Skip Vote:** If the town comes to a unanimous decision early, they can vote to skip the remaining phase time to proceed instantly to the execution.

### 5. ⚖️ The Verdict Phase (6 Seconds)

_The town's decision is final._

- The votes are instantly tallied, and the player with the most votes is executed without mercy.
- Their true role—Innocent or Killer—is immediately revealed!
- In the event of a tie, the town is paralyzed with indecision, and no one hangs.
- _If the Jester is executed, they win immediately!_

## 💬 Chat Systems & Secrets

Information is power, but only if you know who's listening. NightFall features a robust, multi-channel communication system:

- **💬 Public Chat:** Available to all living players during the Grace and Dinner phases. Use it to accuse, defend, and share evidence.
- **🗡 Killer Team Chat:** A hyper-secret channel where Killers can coordinate targets and craft synchronized alibis during _any_ phase. (Be careful, a Detective's Wiretap might intercept your plans!)
- **🔍 Detective Team Chat:** A secure channel exclusively for multiple Detectives to share verified clues and coordinate investigations safely.
- **👻 Ghost Chat:** Death is not the end. Dead players speak to each other in a hidden Ghost Chat to vent and spectate, completely invisible to the living.
- **💀 Last Words:** Executed players may sometimes manage to broadcast a brief, final message to the server right as they are dragged to the gallows.
- **🤫 Whispers:** If enabled by the host, players can send private Direct Messages to one another. **Limitation:** You only get a limited number of whispers per match (usually 2). Choose your confidants carefully so the Killers can't easily gang up in secret!

## ⚡ Quick Time Events (QTEs) & Evidence Mastery

NightFall requires raw skill just as much as social deduction.

### The Escalating Kill QTE

A Killer's job gets significantly harder as the bodies pile up. The Kill QTE permanently escalates in difficulty with each successful murder:

- **1st Kill:** 2 keys (1.8s per key) ➞ _Failing leaves a vague hint._
- **2nd Kill:** 3 keys (1.4s per key) ➞ _Failing drops a Persona trait clue._
- **3rd Kill:** 4 keys (1.1s per key) ➞ _Failing leaves massive physical evidence._
- **4th+ Kill:** 5 keys (0.85s per key) ➞ _Failing leaves damning identification._

**Remember:** If the Killer fails the QTE entirely, the murder botches. **The target survives**, and a huge piece of evidence is left behind.

### Season 2 Ability QTEs

Your special abilities aren't free; they trigger specialized, thematic mini-games. Failing these wastes your limited charges or gives you severely degraded intel:

- **Slider (Autopsy):** Stop a rapidly moving bar perfectly in the sweet spot to dissect the corpse.
- **Jigsaw (Cold Case):** Frantically reconstruct fragmented evidence pieces before the timer expires.
- **Burn (Evidence Destroy):** Rapidly mash the correct inputs to incinerate a clue before anyone notices.
- **Spotlight (Town Watch):** Track elusive, moving targets across the dark manor layout.

## 🏆 Win Conditions

Who walks away from the manor alive?

1. **The Town (Innocents) Wins** if ALL Killers are successfully identified and executed by the gavel during the Day Phase.
2. **The Killers Win** the moment the number of living Killers is equal to or greater than the number of living Innocents.
3. **The Jester Wins** if they trick the town into executing them! (The game continues for everyone else, leaving the Jester to laugh from the grave!)
