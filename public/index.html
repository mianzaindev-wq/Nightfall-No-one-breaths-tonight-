    📜 How to Play

    🎯 The Goal

    Innocents must deduce and execute all Killers. Killers must silence the town until they outnumber the living.

    🌑 The Night (Action Phase)

    Killers choose victims. Innocents choose suspects to investigate. Then, everyone must complete a QTE (Quick Time Event).

    Precise key-presses equal a clean night. Sloppy presses mean you fail your investigation, or worse — leave behind physical evidence if you are the killer.

    ☀️ The Day (Deduction Phase)

    Discuss the night in chat. Evidence dropped by killers will automatically link back to their secret Game Persona (e.g. The Clockmaker).

    Use the Suspicion Heatmap to track voting patterns, and vote to execute the most guilty player before time runs out!

    📋 Town Board — Resident Profiles

    Public descriptions visible to all. Hidden details require investigation.
