    📜 How to Play

    The Game

    NightFall is a social deduction game. Players are secretly assigned roles. Each night, killers strike. Each day, the town discusses and votes to eliminate a suspect. Find the killers before they outnumber you.

    Core Roles

    🗡
    Killer
    Choose a victim each night. Blend in during the day. Win when killers equal or outnumber the living.
    🔍
    Detective
    Investigate one player per night to learn if they are suspicious. Share findings with the town.
    🧑
    Civilian
    No special powers. Observe, discuss, and vote wisely — the town's fate is in your hands.

    Optional Roles

    🩺
    Doctor
    Each night, protect one player. If the killer targets them, they survive. Cannot self-protect two nights in a row.
    🤡
    Jester
    An agent of chaos. The Jester wins the game if they are voted out during the day. The game continues for others.

    Game Flow

    🌙 Night: Killers choose a victim. Detective investigates. Doctor protects.

    ☀ Day: The victim is announced. Players discuss and vote to execute a suspect.

    ⚖ Verdict: The player with the most votes is eliminated. Their role is revealed.

    🔄 Repeat until one side wins.
